What we offer

Each year, netsoc adds to the list of services that we offer our members. In addition to some of the best parties, holidays and nerd-talks, each week we run tutorials on web-design and related tech stuff.

We have a well stocked library with over 100 technical, fictional and cartoon books, which we add to every year. We also have backdated Wired magazines (currently in the JCR).

Our society room is also open to members. You can use our Linux, OpenBSD, Solaris, MacOS and Win2k machines and create DVDs on our DVD/CD burner.

On top of all that, we offer free technical support and tutorials to all members, and have a few new surprises in store for the coming year!
